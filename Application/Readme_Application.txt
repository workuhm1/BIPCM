Description:
In Section 5 of our manuscript "Properties of Ideal Point Classification Models for Bivariate Binary Data. Worku, H.M., and De Rooij, M. Psychometrika (Accepted for publication, 2016)", the (B)IPC models are applied to real data from Netherlands Study of Depression and Anxiety (NESDA: Penninx et al, 2008). The NESDA is a psychological data relating personality traits to depression and anxiety disorders.  

In case you would like to know more about NESDA data, please contact the second author.
